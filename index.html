About

I am a PhD student in Applied Economics at the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania. My research is in the fields of environmental economics and development economics.

Prior to graduate school, I received a B.Eng. Sc. in Industrial Engineering and an M.A. in Economics at Universidad de Chile.
